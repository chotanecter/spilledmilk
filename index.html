Cast

The faces behind Spilled Milk. Tap any card to read more.

J Alphonse Nicholson
as Trey Carver
One of the most magnetic actors working today. Best known for his role as Lil Murda on the Starz drama P-Valley, Nicholson is a two-time NAACP Image Award nominee whose credits include Just Mercy, They Cloned Tyrone, and a Broadway debut in A Soldier's Play. He is also an accomplished percussionist and emerging producer/director.
More
Jerry O'Connell
as The Milk Man
A household name and one of Hollywood's most enduring talents. O'Connell has been acting since age eleven, when he starred in Stand by Me. His career spans Jerry Maguire, Scream 2, Kangaroo Jack, the sci-fi series Sliders, and five seasons voicing Commander Jack Ransom on Star Trek: Lower Decks. He also appeared on Broadway in A Soldier's Play alongside Nicholson.
More
Rodney Van Johnson
as Marcus Carver
A true renaissance man with one of the most unlikely paths to Hollywood. A former world-class track and field athlete and Olympic qualifier in the 100 meters, Johnson became the only actor to appear consecutively on three different soap operas across three networks. He is a direct descendant of Oliver Lewis, the jockey who won the first Kentucky Derby.
More
Brandon Perea
as Jose
The definition of a scene-stealer. Perea broke out as Angel Torres in Jordan Peele's Nope, where Peele rewrote the script to expand the character around what Perea brought to his audition. The performance earned him a BAFTA Breakthrough Artist selection. He currently stars in the latest installment of the Insidious franchise and can be seen in Doe, Kid Cudi's feature directorial debut.
More
Scott "Kid Cudi" Mescudi
as Barry
A man who needs no introduction, Scott is a talented multi-hyphenate who has never stopped evolving. The Grammy-winning artist has built a film career spanning Ti West's X, Adam McKay's Don't Look Up, and Happy Gilmore 2. In addition to acting in Spilled Milk, Mescudi composed the film's original score.
More
Directed By
Jared Leaf
Writer, director, and producer behind Spilled Milk. The film premiered at TIFF, won the Audience Award at the Pan African Film Festival, and was selected as an HBO Short Film Award nominee.
